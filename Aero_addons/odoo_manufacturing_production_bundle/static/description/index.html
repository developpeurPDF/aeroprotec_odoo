Engineering Change Order (ECO) for BOM

Engineering Change Order (ECO) for BOM

An engineering change order (ECO) is a documentation packet that outlines the proposed change of Bill of material for product / items.

For More Details: http://www.odoo.com/apps/modules/16.0/bom_change_version

Process Costing in Manufacturing Process

Process Costing in Manufacturing Process

This app allow you to do process costing (Material Cost, Labour Cost, Overheads) for manufacturing orders.

Material Requisitions from Manufacturing / Workorder

Material Requisitions from Manufacturing / Workorder

Allow your production team to Create Material Requisition directly from Manufacturing order.

Process Costing Manufacturing for By-Products

Process Costing Manufacturing for By-Products

This app allow you to do process costing (Material Cost, Labour Cost, Overheads) for By-Products in manufacturing orders.

Print Manufacturing Order Barcode/Lot

Print Manufacturing Order Barcode/Lot

This module print lot barcode from Manufacturing Order for Raw materials and Finished product.

Manufacturing WorkOrder Integration Task

Manufacturing WorkOrder Integration Task

This module will create tasks from manufacturing work-orders.

BOM Product Cost Price

BOM Product Cost Price

This module allow you to view BOM cost Price on Product which has BOM and Show Total Cost on BOM Form.

Product BOM (Bill of Material) Duplication

Product BOM (Bill of Material) Duplication

This app allow you to manage Product BOM Duplication when you duplicate product and product variants. This app can be useful when you have many number of Bill of Materials for the Products and Variants and Product duplication happen many cases. Bill of Materials BOM will copy automatically to the new Product which are duplicated.
