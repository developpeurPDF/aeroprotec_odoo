Process Costing Manufacturing for By Products

Manufacturing Process Costing App in Odoo

Material Cost / Labour Cost / Overhead Cost on Bill of Material for by Products

Manufacturing Order by By Products Process Costing in Odoo

This app allow you to do process costing (Material Cost, Labour Cost, Overheads) for By-Products in manufacturing orders.


Main Features:
  • Configure Direct material cost for By-Products, Direct labour cost for By-Products, Overhead cost for By-Products on Bill of Materials
  • Manufacturing Order with Direct material cost for By-Products, Direct labour cost for By-Products, Overhead cost for By-Products from Bill of Materials.
  • When you create Manufacturing Order and select BOM then system will automatically fill all Costing you have configured on BOM into Manufacturing order by products tab. You can allow to update Actual qty anytime for by-product costing manually.
  • When you update Qty of your Manufacturing order it will auto update all By product cost lines based on that changes in qty.
  • Print Process costing by Product report from BOM and Manufacturing order.
  • This app does NOT create any accounting entry for costing of manufacturing of By-products.
